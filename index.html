Introducing Trill, the app allowing young children to learn how to read classical music in a fun and engaging mini-course.

Aims

The app aims to provide an interactive and entertaining way of learning musical terms and notation.

It’s really hard to find teaching materials for children that aren’t boring, unimaginative books - often focusing on function more than form. But the facts show that children will not engage with learning materials in a productive way without catering to their developing minds.

Meet the Creator

“Years ago when I was learning the basics of music like symbols and notation, I found the process really monotonous and that really put a stop to feeling good about reading music from the sheet. I started ‘ear-reading’ as they call it, and my efficiency when learning new pieces suffered as a result.

So I started thinking about more engaging ways to memorise the boring stuff and I thought, “A lot more children these days have smartphones, tablets and other devices than when I was learning… Why not make an app?”.”

-Aislinn Milner, Founder
